Everything you need to know about investing in real estate NFTs

How does real estate NFT investment work? +
Each NFT represents a €1,000 fractional ownership stake in the property. When you purchase an NFT, you own a proportional share and receive rental income distributions. All ownership and income distribution is managed transparently through smart contracts.
What are the expected returns? +
Based on current German mixed-use property markets, we project 5-7% annual yields. This includes rental income from both residential and commercial tenants, distributed monthly in USDC stablecoin directly to your wallet.
How many NFTs are available and at what price? +
There are 2,500 NFTs total, each priced at €1,000. This represents the full €2.5M fundraising goal to acquire the €1.795M property and cover formation costs, legal fees, and initial operating capital.
Is this investment regulated? +
Yes, CoinEstate operates under Estonian financial regulations as an EU-licensed entity. All property acquisitions follow German real estate law, and our smart contracts have been independently audited for security and compliance.
What are the fees and minimum investment? +
The minimum investment is €1,000 (1 NFT). We charge a 2% annual management fee, deducted from rental income before distribution. There are no hidden fees, exit penalties, or additional charges for income distributions.
How do I receive rental income? +
Rental income is distributed monthly directly to your wallet in USDC stablecoin. You can claim your proportional share at any time through our platform or directly from the smart contract with no additional fees.
Can I sell my NFTs before the property is sold? +
Yes, NFTs can be traded on secondary markets or through our planned marketplace. This provides liquidity compared to traditional real estate investments, though market demand may vary and affect pricing.
