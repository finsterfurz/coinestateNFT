💎 Non-Financial Digital Assets - Community Access Only

CoinEstate NFTs are non-financial digital assets. They do not constitute securities under EU financial regulations, as they confer no entitlement to revenue, dividends, or equity. NFTs provide community access and participation rights only, with no financial returns or profit-sharing provided or implied.

How It Works

Three simple steps to join the future of legally-compliant real estate community membership

1

Mint NFT Membership

Purchase an NFT to join a specific real estate community. Each property has its own limited collection, granting exclusive access and lifelong participation rights in that community. NFTs include timestamped mint records for full transparency.

2

Access Gated Property Insights

Access gated, non-commercial property insights and dashboards for informational purposes only. Track property performance through detailed analytics, renovation updates, and market insights with blockchain-verified reporting.

3

Non-binding Community Participation

Non-binding participation in community discussions about future property projects. Advisory voting only with no financial consequence or decision-making power, ensuring full legal compliance.
